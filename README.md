# 🛒 Big Bazar Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

A comprehensive **Power BI Dashboard** built to analyze and visualize Big Bazar's sales performance. This dashboard provides interactive insights into revenue, product performance, payment methods, department-wise sales, and monthly sales trends, enabling better business decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Power BI visualizations and DAX measures. It helps stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and identify sales trends across different business dimensions.

## 📌 Business Insights

- Generated over **₹845 Million** in revenue.
- Cash payments contributed the highest revenue.
- Furniture and Skin Care products were among the top-performing products.
- Revenue remained relatively stable with peaks in January and March.
- Department-wise revenue distribution was balanced across all categories.
